# YC engine — FIA empirical yield-curve engine (cls `YC`)

Adds a new engine class, **`YC`**, to the PERSEUS multi-model series for the
focal states (ME, IN, GA). The engine projects each state's *current FIA
inventory* forward along empirical yield curves fitted directly from the FIA
chronosequence — no FVS/process model required — under two scenarios with a
climate-sensitivity ribbon.

## Method
1. **Stratification** (`ycx_00_strata.R`). Each FIA plot is assigned to a
   stratum of **forest-type group × EPA Level III ecoregion × ownership**.
   - forest-type group: FIA `FORTYPCD` collapsed to its national group code.
   - province: EPA Level III ecoregion (`NA_L3CODE`), spatial join on plot
     `LAT`/`LON`.
   - ownership: USFS CONUS forest-ownership raster (Harris/Caputo/Butler 2025,
     RDS-2025-0045) — Family→NIPF, Corporate→Industrial, State→State,
     Federal/Local/Tribal→Public-Other; FIA `OWNGRPCD` fallback where the
     raster reads Unknown/Non-Forest/Water at the (fuzzed) plot point.
2. **Curves** (`ycx_01_curves.R`). Bounded Chapman-Richards fits per
   (stratum × treatment × response) over stand age, for untreated and
   harvested plots, with a cell → ft×owner → ft → state fallback hierarchy.
   Harvested asymptotes are anchored to the untreated carrying capacity when
   they exceed it by >20% (the Maine v4 logic, generalized).
3. **Projection → calendar-year totals** (`ycx_02_perseus.R` +
   `ycx_merge_perseus.py`). Every forested plot is advanced from its current
   age; the state mean density trajectory is anchored to the FIA above-ground
   live carbon total (`fia.json` `tg_agc`) so both scenarios start at the
   observed 2025 stock and diverge by their forward increments:
   - `reserve (no harvest)` — untreated curve (passive succession)
   - `managed (harvest)` — anchored harvested curve
   - climate sensitivity — ±10% productivity ribbon (`pts = [year, mean, lo, hi]`).

## Scope / caveats
- Pilot states ME, IN, GA; metric `agc_live_total`.
- "Managed" reflects the empirical harvested-stand trajectory, not an explicit
  rotation/removal schedule; it is a first-order managed scenario.
- Plot measurement-year offsets are collapsed to a common 2025 baseline.
- Source pipeline runs on OSC Cardinal (`~/yield_curves_conus/`); scripts are
  mirrored under `scripts/yield_curve_engine/` for reproducibility.
